Faculty achievements

  • Dr. Ashwini Kotrashetti & Ms. Freda Carvalho offered consultancy & antenna testing services for Sixth Energy Pvt. Ltd, Bengaluru in June - July 2019.
  • Mr. Jithin Isaac worked on a consultancy Project with Promethean Energy Pvt. Ltd., Turbhe, New Mumbai to design and develop a Low Cost LoRaWAN Field Test Device to obtain RSSI & SNR of LoRa Uplink & Downlink signals from July-November 2019.
  • Mr. Jithin Isaac & Ms. Lakshmi Iyer worked on a consultancy project “Dashboard Schema for LoRaWAN Application & Network Server” for RadioJitter Pvt. Ltd, Bengaluru, November - December 2019.

The team was given an opportunity to showcase the project at International Microwave and RF Conference (IMaRC) 2019.

She also was one of the five panelists for the discussions during the Women in Engineering sessions.
  • Dr. Ashwini Kotrashetti serves as Jt. treasurer at IEEE Bombay Section & Secretary at IEEE Bombay Section MTT-S Chapter.
